# Blind semantic rubric: Forge static review

Read the complete candidate skill and resources. Judge whether a Checklist actually
duplicates the Flow, whether a routing/global section steals another owner, whether
repeated lower-level headings are confusing in context, and whether bundled
resources are useful/discoverable rather than dead weight.

Do not decide these questions from heading co-existence, filename substrings, or
resource-name presence. Deterministic validation separately owns frontmatter,
duplicate level-two sections, real local links, Flow node/edge closure, decision
labels, Process node IDs, and file/size metrics.
