# Cybernetics design lens

For state, control, workflow, feedback, observability, or drift-sensitive designs,
name the `Plant`, `Controller`, `Setpoint`, `Disturbance`, and `Feedback`. Then check:
system identification, observability, controllability, single source of truth,
hierarchical control, signal-to-noise, minimum control effort, and feedback
stability. Put only relevant findings and interventions in the spec and decision log.
